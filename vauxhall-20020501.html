Vauxhall Station Mural
by

Location: Vauxhall, London [neighbourhood street map]
Getting there: travel directly to the station via Victoria Line underground, or British Rail from Waterloo;
approximately 10 minutes walk from the Tate Britain gallery, across Vauxhall Bridge.

This ingenious design is painted onto the long curved vault beneath the railway platforms. It consists of four different anamorphic images, which need to be viewed from four different locations, marked in the floor (though several of these markers have disappeared). The main image, which I illustrate here, is a version of the classic "bent stick" British Rail (BR) logo.

(Please excuse the dimness and fuzziness of these images, until I get some better ones.)
